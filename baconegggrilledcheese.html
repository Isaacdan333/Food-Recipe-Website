Bacon and Egg Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  2. In the same skillet, remove some of the excess bacon grease, leaving about a teaspoon in the pan.
  3. Crack the eggs into the skillet and cook to your desired level of doneness (e.g., sunny-side-up or scrambled). Season with salt and pepper.
  4. While the eggs are cooking, spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread.
  6. Place the cooked bacon on top of the cheese.
  7. Once the eggs are done, carefully transfer them onto the bacon.
  8. Top the eggs with the second slice of cheese and the other slice of bread, buttered side up.
  9. Return the sandwich to the skillet and cook on medium-low heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, flipping halfway through.
  10. Remove from the skillet, let it cool for a minute, then slice in half and serve.

Enjoy your delicious bacon and egg grilled cheese sandwich!
